# Oat milk 1

## Ingredients

- 3 cups of cold water
- handful of ice cubes
- 1 cup oats
- 1/4th cup of cashews or walnuts
- pinch of salt
- few drops of vanilla

## Recipe

- Blend all on high for 30s
- Strain through nut milk bag
